What is this all about?

Fostering innovation and entrepreneurship across IISERs through collaborative events

Hosted by EIC IISER Pune, the E-Summit fosters innovation and entrepreneurship across all IISERs. Our multi-day event unites entrepreneurial cells, celebrating science and entrepreneurship. This summit shows that students from India's top institutes can excel in the entrepreneurial landscape. We challenge stereotypes, proving that science and entrepreneurship go hand in hand, inspiring peers to see it as a path to impact.
